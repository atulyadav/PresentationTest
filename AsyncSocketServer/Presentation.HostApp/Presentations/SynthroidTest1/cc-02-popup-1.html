CARR Study Summary

Following a 25-mcg dose change of
levothyroxine, most patients had changes
in TSH levels2

Of the 21 patients who took part in this study,
all were treated with medication for their
hypothyroidism.

  • Ten patients were dosed 25 mcg higher than
    their optimal dose.
  • Two patients were dosed 25 mcg lower than their
    optimal dose.
  • Nine patients were dosed 25 mcg higher and
    lower than their optimal dose.
