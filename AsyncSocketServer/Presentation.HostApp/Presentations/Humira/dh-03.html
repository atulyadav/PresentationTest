Pregnant women

Safety Considerations

KALETRA is Pregnancy Category C.

There are no adequate and well-controlled studies of KALETRA in pregnant women.

KALETRA should be used during pregnancy only if potential benefit justifies the potential risks to the fetus.

Mothers should be instructed not to breast-feed if they are receiving KALETRA.

Antiretroviral Pregnancy Registry:
To monitor maternal-fetal outcomes of pregnant women exposed to KALETRA, an Antiretroviral Pregnancy Registry has been established. Physicians are encouraged to register patients by calling 1-800-258-4263 or by going online to apregistry.com.
