Adapted from the Department of Health and Human Services Pregnancy Guidelines3

Other classes of ARV agents are listed as recommended in the DHHS Guidelines for Treating
HIV-Infected Pregnant Women. For more information, go to www.aidsinfo.nih.gov/guidelines/.

According to the DHHS Guidelines, therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM) in addition to clinical assessment
integrated with other clinical information, may be useful in pregnant women. Physicians should consult
the most current literature when using a TDM strategy.2
