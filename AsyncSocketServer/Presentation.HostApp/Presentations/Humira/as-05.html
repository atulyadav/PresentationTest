ARTEMIS: Lipid analyses
Mean fasting lipid levels at baseline and week 962,5

Patients receiving DRV/RTV had smaller
median increases than KALETRA in
triglycerides (8.9 vs 53.4 mg/dL) and
total cholesterol (23.4 vs 35.1 mg/dL).

A similar proportion of patients used
lipid-lowering agents in the DRV/RTV
arm (7%) and the KALETRA arm (8%).2

Safety Considerations

Treatment with KALETRA has resulted
in large increases in concentrations
of total cholesterol and triglycerides.
Monitor lipids prior to therapy and
periodically thereafter.

KALETRA is contraindicated with
lovastatin or simvastatin.

Treat patients for lipid elevations
as clinically appropriate.
