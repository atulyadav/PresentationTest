SWITCHMRK 1 and 2

Switch to a raltegravir-based regimen versus continuation of a KALETRA-based regimen in stable HIV-1 infected patients with suppressed viremia: two double-blind, randomized controlled trials2

Primary endpoints

Proportion of patients with viral load <50 copies/mL at week 24.
Non-completer=Failure approach (NC=F).
Noninferiority demonstrated if the lower bound of the 95% confidence interval for the difference between treatment arms was less than 12%.
Mean % change in fasting lipids at week 12.
Total-C, triglycerides, non-HDL-C, and LDL-C.
Frequency of adverse events up to 24 weeks.
