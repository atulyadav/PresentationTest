KALETRA can be taken with or without food.

KALETRA can be coadministered with acid-reducing agents with no dose adjustment.

Food restrictions
Co-administration of KALETRA is contraindicated with drugs that are highly dependent on CYP3A
for clearance (possibly resulting in serious and/or life-threatening events) and potent CYP3A
inducers (which may be associated with loss of virologic response, resistance and cross-resistance).
