Study 720
7-year Open-Label Extension (OLE) KALETRA study in naïve patients

A phase 2, dose-ranging, randomized, multicenter trial evaluating the safety and efficacy of KALETRA soft-gel capsules with stavudine and lamivudine
at 3 dose levels within 2 groups of treatment-naïve patients with confirmed
HIV-1 infection. The study consisted of a double-blind study period and an
open-label study period.3,4

Primary endpoint

Proportion of patients with plasma HIV-1 RNA <400 copies/mL or <50 copies/mL at week 24
and the duration of virologic response through week 48.

The study was extended with an open-label follow-up period through 360 weeks.
