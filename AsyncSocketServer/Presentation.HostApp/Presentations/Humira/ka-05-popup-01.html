KALETRA is contraindicated in patients who have hypersenstivity to the drug or its ingredients. Co-administration of KALETRA is contraindicated with drugs that are highly dependent on CYP3A for clearance (possibly resulting in serious and/or life-threatening events) and potent CYP3A inducers (which may be associated with loss of virologic response, resistance and cross-resistance).

*Department of Health and Human Services.
