7 years of data in treatment- naïve patients

59% of ITT patients (n=100) had undetectable viral loads at year 7.

95% of patients who remained in the study (n=62) had undetectable viral
loads at year 7.

Total discontinuation rate: 39% (16% discontinued due to adverse events, 10% due to other reasons, 4% due to non-compliance and 9% were lost to follow-up).

Safety Considerations

In KALETRA clinical trials, the most common adverse reactions reported in >5% of adult patients were diarrhea, nausea, abdominal pain, asthenia, vomiting, headache, and dyspepsia. In Study 720, lipodystrophy was also reported.
