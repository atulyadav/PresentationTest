Safety Considerations

Do not administer once-daily KALETRA in adult patients with three or more lopinavir resistance-associated substitutions; in combination with efavirenz, nevirapine, nelfinavir, carbamazepine, phenobarbital, or phenytoin; or in pediatric patients.
Patients should be informed that there may be greater chance of developing diarrhea with the once-daily regimen as compared with the twice-daily regimen.
