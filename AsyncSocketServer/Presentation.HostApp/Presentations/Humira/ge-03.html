Study 730: Viral load suppression was similar
between QD and BID arms
Proportion of patients with HIV-1 RNA <50 copies/mL
through week 48 (ITT, NC=F and OT analysis)6
ITT, intent-to-treat; OT, on-treatment; NC=F,
non-completer equals failure.

Subject Disposition

In the QD group, 4.8% of patients
discontinued treatment due to
adverse events, 0.6% due to death, 0.6% due to virologic failure, 12% for other reasons*; 85.3% of patients
completed the study.

In the BID group, 3% of patients
discontinued treatment due to adverse events, 0.3% due to death, 1.5% due to virologic failure, 14% for other reasons*; 83.4% of patients completed the study.

Virologic failure through week 48: 10%
in the QD group and 8% in the BID group. Virologic failure includes
confirmed viral rebound and failure to achieve confirmed HIV RNA <50 copies/mL through week 48.

*Other reasons include lost to follow-up, non-adherence, withdrawal of consent, and other. Subjects/investigators may have provided more than one reason for discontinuation.
