Safety Considerations

In ARTEMIS, the most common moderate to severe treatment related events occurring in  ≥2% of KALETRA patients were diarrhea and nausea.3 Other common adverse events reported in KALETRA clinical trials include abdominal pain, vomiting, asthenia, dyspepsia, and headache.1
