Safety Considerations

Treatment with KALETRA has resulted in large increases in concentrations of total cholesterol and triglycerides. Monitor lipids prior to therapy and periodically thereafter.
KALETRA is contraindicated with lovastatin and simvastatin.

SWITCHMRK 1 and 2 results

The raltegravir group experienced significantly greater percentage reductions in fasting triglycerides, total cholesterol, and non-HDL cholesterol from baseline to week 12 compared to the KALETRA group.
