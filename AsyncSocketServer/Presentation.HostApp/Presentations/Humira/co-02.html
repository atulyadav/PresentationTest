Safety Considerations

Do not administer once-daily KALETRA in adult patients with three or more lopinavir resistance-associated substitutions.

KALETRA should not be administered once daily in combination with efavirenz, nevirapine, nelfinavir, carbamazepine,
phenobarbital, or phenytoin.

KALETRA tablets should not be administered once daily in pediatric patients <18 years of age.

Patients should be informed that there may be a greater chance of developing
diarrhea with the once-daily regimen as compared with the twice-daily regimen.
