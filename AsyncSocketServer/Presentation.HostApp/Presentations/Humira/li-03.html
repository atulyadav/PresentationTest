Safety Considerations

Treatment with KALETRA has resulted in large increases in concentrations of total cholesterol
and triglycerides. Monitor lipids prior to therapy and periodically thereafter.

KALETRA is contraindicated with lovastatin and simvastatin.

Patients should be treated as clinically appropriate.
